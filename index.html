Off-Earth Manufacturing

Off-Earth manufacturing utilizes in-situ resources on the Moon, Mars, and asteroids to build habitats, tools, and infrastructure directly in space.

Technologies like 3D printing with regolith and ISRU reduce launch costs and enable sustainable colonization far from Earth.

Building humanity's future homes among the stars, one local material at a time.
